Instructions

This application is a step-by-step evaluator for a dialect of microKanren.

Goals

The central pane shows the current goal. Highlighted areas of the goal text can be clicked to evaluate the next step.

Some goals can be evaluated in only one way, and some goals can be evaluated in multiple (or zero) ways.

For example, at a disjunction or a conjunction, either of the two subgoals can be evaluated.

At a conjunction, one subgoal is evaluated first, and the other subgoal is added to the list of remaining goals, displayed in the bottom left pane. When evaluation completes, the first remaining goal will be evaluated. When there are no more remaining goals, evaluation is complete for the original goal.

Substitutions

The current substitution is shown in the top left pane. As goals are evaluated, the substitution will grow, until a solution is found or a contradiction is met.

The substitution grows when two terms are unified.
